Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Bemonk is a focus tracking web application available at bemonk.app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, how it's stored, and your rights. We keep this short and honest.

1. Who we are

Bemonk is an independent product. For questions about this policy, contact us at support@bemonk.app.

2. What we collect

We collect only what's needed to run the app:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account.
  • Password — stored as a secure hash. We never see your plain-text password.
  • Focus session data — the number of flows you complete each day, time spent, and your session history.
  • App settings — your timer durations, preferences, and display name.
  • Pro/payment status — whether you have an active Pro account or trial, and when it was activated. We do not store card numbers.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, IP address, or any data beyond what's listed above.

3. How we store it

All account data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted database platform with encrypted storage and row-level security — meaning your data is only accessible by you. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant and stores data in secure US-based data centers.

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage and are never readable by anyone, including us.

4. Payments

Bemonk Pro is a one-time $7 lifetime purchase processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. Bemonk never sees, stores, or touches your card number, CVV, or billing address. All payment data is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We store only whether your payment was successful and when — not the card details themselves.

5. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to run the app:

  • To display your focus history, stats, streaks, and charts
  • To sync your data across browsers and devices when you're logged in
  • To send password reset emails when you request them
  • To verify your Pro access when you log in

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with third parties except Supabase (storage) and Stripe (payments).

6. Emails we send

We send only two types of emails:

  • Account confirmation — sent once when you sign up.
  • Password reset — sent only when you request it.

Support emails are handled via ImprovMX, a mail forwarding service, which routes messages sent to support@bemonk.app to our inbox. We do not send marketing emails or newsletters.

7. Cookies and tracking

Bemonk does not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. Supabase stores a session token in your browser to keep you logged in. This is a functional cookie required for the app to work — it does not track you across other websites.

8. Data retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all associated data — your email, session history, settings, and Pro status — is permanently deleted.

9. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (contact us and we'll provide it)

To exercise any of these rights, email support@bemonk.app. We'll respond within 7 days.

10. Children

Bemonk is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Bemonk after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions? Email us at support@bemonk.app — we'll get back to you.
